It must be possible for the customer to increase the credit register again by loading credit.
With credit "0", the meter should switch off within the legally permissible time range. It must be possible to enter the meter to load credit before activating the dept function.
A configurable credit function should also be activatable. After confirmation by the customer should this Credit framework become active. The meter is then to be unlocked for on-site reconnection, and the customer has the possibility within the credit line still to draw electrical energy.
Deactivation is done in the reverse order.

Customers are registered by the central system as debt customers in compliance with legal framework conditions. The central system initiates the corresponding reparameterization of the meter from normal operation to dept operation.

The meter works in prepayment mode. A credit register is visible to the customer on the display, at least in kWh (optionally also in euro). The credit will be counted down to 0.
